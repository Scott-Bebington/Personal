Percentage Splits

eg. If the markets produce a 22% return for the year, the split will be as follows (Tier 4 client with $5 000 investment):

for the first 10% return, the split will be 70/30 (70% to the investor and 30% to Bebvest)
for the next 12% return, the split will be 60/40 (60% to the investor and 40% to Bebvest)
... and so on.
this is not the same as saying that the investor will receive 60% of the total return, but rather that the split will be 60/40 for the next 12% return

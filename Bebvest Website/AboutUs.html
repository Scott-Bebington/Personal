Gary Bebington

CEO & Founder

Gary’s career started out as a software engineer working for various large corporates including Global companies such as Accenture and Dimension Data. He won a global award for implementing the largest BI solution in the Southern Hemisphere on a Microsoft platform to name but a few achievements. Gary then had the good fortune to meet and work with Courtney Smith, a world renowned hedge fund trader/manager and stock picker. His achievements are virtually unparalled in the financial world. In that tenure Gary learnt many trade secrets, trade techniques and money management rules that few are privy to. This paved the way to design and develop an unique trading algorithm that incorporates many techniques and money management strategies. Gary continues to enhance the algorithm to not only deliver stellar returns, but minimise risk in the process.

Duard Botha

Chief Architect

Duard obtained his Bachelor of Science (BSc) focused in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Johannesburg (RAU). He has over 30 years of experience as program manager, technology specialist and architect in the financial services industry. Duard specialised as an Enterprise Architect and IT Strategy Consultant for various blue chip companies in the last few years. He is currently the Chief Architect for Sportbot Holdings International, POLC Group IT (UAE) and Bebvest (UAE).

Dr Johan Bosch

Bebvest CMO & Founder POLC Global

Johan is the Group CEO and Founder of POLC Global and Sportbot Holdings International as well as CMO for Bebvest. Johan holds a doctorate in Expert Support Systems from the University of Johannesburg. He spent several years as an advisory consultant specializing in future-proof IT Strategy, IT Governance, Risk, Compliance, and Security for PwC, Ernst & Young, and Deloitte and ran several programs for ABSA Bank, Barclays, Standard Bank, and Discovery Bank. Some of his experience includes the role of Group CIO of a multi-national bank in Africa. For the last few years, he consulted as CTO for two of the largest international BPOs in South Africa. Johan is a thought leader in future-proof Fintech companies and financial switching systems using artificial intelligence or human expert support systems. Johan is deeply passionate about using artificial intelligence and expert support systems to lift people globally out of poverty with the companies patented technology.

Investment Rules

Our Rules!

The business rules described below are in no order of precedence or sequence:

  • Minimum deposit requirement is $3000
  • Initial deposit must be held for a minimum period of one year
  • Subsequent deposit minimums are $1500 provided original investment is still in play
  • Subsequent deposits must be held for a minimum of 1 year
  • Payments can be made any time within the month
  • All monthly deposit payments will be transferred to the trading fund at the end of each month
  • Monthly updates will be provided to each client via email
  • Proceed pay-outs will happen half-yearly either end of February or end of August
  • The pay-outs will happen on the above dates provided it surpasses the fund's first anniversary
  • Deposits will be valued as tokens and each token will represent $10 (tokens are used for investment as a common currency regardless of originating denomination)
  • Growth will be evaluated in additional tokens earned
  • 30 days' notice must be given if withdrawal of funds is required provided it satisfies the minimum 1-year rule
